Flaggi Images

These images can be used as a Flaggi. You can spawn the from your Admin Menu under "Building".
You can set the orientation and direction you want. Then you can open them up and select a URL to use.
Below are the samples created based on the colors of your Capitol Building colors and lighting.


You can right click the images and "copy image address" then paste into the URL spot inside the Flaggi.
The reversed images are for if the Flaggi orientation is backwards.
